Origins of Crash Games

Crash games didn't emerge from traditional casino game development. Their origins are rooted in cryptocurrency gambling communities.

The crash game concept emerged from Bitcoin gambling sites in the mid-2010s. The first known crash game implementations appeared around 2014, offering a simple but compelling premise:

  • Cash out before it crashes
  • Provably fair verification using blockchain

Bustabit: The Pioneer

One of the most influential early crash games was Bustabit, launched in 2014. Bustabit established many conventions that still define crash games:

  • Simple visual interface with rising multiplier

Why Crash Games Emerged

Crash games filled a niche in cryptocurrency gambling:
